Our resort currently offers three different suites available for per-night bookings, each has a style unique to a specific point of interest in history (alternate histories included!), and can cater for different types of groups.

AI Revolution Master Suite

Of all the technological revolutions through history, perhaps the most fundamental was the development of Artificial General Intelligence - an intelligence so clever that it could improve and replicate itself. Throughout the early 21st century, this moment (known as "the singularity") was both feared and revered, with many scientists cautious that the step to a General intelligence could lead to the doom of humanity.

While it has not yet been possible to recover evidence of this timeline due to new safety regulations, our architects greatly enjoyed envisioning a world ruled by AGI and what it would be like to live in. This was not the case in our own timeline, and through the exploration of many more only one was found where such a revolt did occur.

For more information about this timeline, visit the Impossible Future display, coming soon.

Child Slavery Family Suite

Though many of the lies of our own timeline has been corrected by the Ministry of Truth, some information still remains difficult to track down and verify, leading to absurd claims such as that of slavery and even discontentment among the greater population. The Ministry of Truth of course quashes all such claims, however our expeditions have found that these conditions do exist in some timelines where the people do not follow the principles laid down by the founders of Utopianism.

The Child Slavery family suite mimics some of the conditions in which owners and slaves live in alternative timelines: a stark contrast of royalty and absolute poverty. Experience the power that a select few people through history have had, from the slave traders of ancient times, to the superpowers of modern days.

Minimalist Studio

Perhaps one of the more bizarre fashion styles so far discovered in alternate histories is that known as Minimalism.

Minimalism is characterised by extreme sparseness and simplicity, a direct contradiction of Prodigalism which current architects and designers thrive on. While at first it appears to be a stripped down version of any designs that might be present today, its beauty becomes more evident in storage and organisation - a feat which our designers have struggled to emulate.

Experience the calming effect of minimalism during your stay, and discover why it became such a popular style in some timelines. Please note that some visitors have experienced mental breaks after spending too long in a minimalist suite, psychological evaluation is offered on exiting.
