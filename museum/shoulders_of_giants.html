On The Shoulders Of Giants

Over the course of human history, many great discoveries have been based on the work of previous authors: be it science, warfare, or literature, the evolution of human knowledge is incremental but each step is even more powerful than hte last.

Th is exhibit aims to explore the evolution of sciences and their impact on the fate of humanity through our history, and alternate histories alike. Though we know our history to be one of noble sacrifice and recovery from the third and final World War, other timelines never saw a third war, and others never reached the 20th century.
