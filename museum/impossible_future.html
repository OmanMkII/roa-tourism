An Impossible Future Exhibit

With many new timelines discovered, subtle and surprising differences have been found between them and our current time. Each of these differences often involves intriguing new technologies that were ignored by our own scientists, and whole schools of thought have been uncovered in exploration and research.

In some timelines technology has evolved at much faster rates than those with less conflict, and allowed for the evolution of advanced artificial intelligence - some of which is not friendly. In others, humans have ventured deep into the known universe and discovered other forms of life not unlike our own, with varying levels of intelligence that have helped each other evolve.
