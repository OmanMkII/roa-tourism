Millenia Of War Exhibit

Experience the many wars that were waged through the ages: from glittering silver chain-mail of medieval knights, to the grey khaki of post-revolution militia, soldiers have fought for many causes before finally looking to the stars. This exhibit explores the evolution of warfare, with artifacts recovered from many time periods to showcase the ingenuity of soldiers and generals alike.

Perhaps the most studied period thus far is the fracture caused by an intervention into the timeline during the 20th century causing the assassination of Adolf Hitler, which quashed World War Two after the initial rise of the Axis powers. Some historians have argued that this event, despite resolving a significant world war, may have sparked the arms race for nuclear weapons and the following nuclear fallout that resulted in near extinction at the climax of World War Three.
