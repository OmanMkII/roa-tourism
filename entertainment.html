Entertainment

Some restaurants and other facilities are currently offered in the main tourism section of the complex, and over the coming months the area will be expanded to accommodate the rapidly growing popularity of this centre.

Out Of This World Dining

Featuring the finest cooks of any lunar residence, this restaurant was the first official restaurant located on the Moon.

Chefs travel here from all around Earth to experience some of the dishes discovered in alternate timelines, such as the incredibly popular "Fried Cardboard & Salt" lovingly copied from the thriving restaurant chain McDonald's.

Reviews:

"Good food, but lacking atmosphere, 4/5 stars."

Bits & Pieces Cyber Cafe

For our cyborg visitors, the Bits & Pieces Cyber Cafe offers multiple flavours of both binary and hexadecimal input streams to keep your sensors primed and ready to go. With several awards on their shelves including Most Expensive Algorithm and Truly Random Data, the unknown owner is a celebrity in the datastream business.

Rumoured to be run by an AI from an alternate timeline, this small cafe has become one of the most popular tourist destinations for humans and mechanoids alike.

Lost & Found Childcare

With some popular destinations being recently ruled too dangerous for minors, the Ministry of Tourism has sought to provide childcare facilities for adults venturing into dangerous timelines.

With highly advanced facilities including automated fetch and devices with the latest edition of Annoyed Pigeons, your children will be pleasantly entertained. The centre also features automated drop off and pick up points, making it even easier to drop children off for the day - and don't worry, we don't charge extra if you forget about them!
